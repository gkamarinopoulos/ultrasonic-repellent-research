# Experiment Notes

## Objective
To investigate the effectiveness of ultrasonic signals for pest repulsion in open-field environments.

---

## Frequency Range
- Target range: 20 kHz – 50 kHz
- Initial focus: 30 kHz – 40 kHz (commonly referenced in literature)

---

## Signal Types
The system supports different modulation strategies:

- Continuous wave (fixed frequency)
- Frequency sweeps (varying frequency over time)
- Pulsed signals
- Randomized frequency variation

Goal: prevent pest adaptation to constant signals.

---

## Expected Behavior
- Certain insect species may be sensitive to ultrasonic frequencies
- Modulated signals are expected to be more effective than static tones
- Environmental conditions will significantly affect performance

---

## Key Variables to Test
- Frequency (kHz)
- Signal pattern (continuous vs modulated)
- Distance from source
- Environmental conditions (humidity, temperature, wind)

---

## Measurement Plan
- Test range every 5 meters
- Compare different frequencies at equal power
- Evaluate consistency over time

---
